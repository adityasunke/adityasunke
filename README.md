## ⚛️ About

CS junior at **Virginia Tech** — B.S. Computer Science with a minor in Quantum Information Science and Engineering (GPA: 3.81). I build algorithms at the intersection of quantum computing and real-world systems: currently leading quantum algorithm development at **CarbonQapture**, a VT-based startup applying hybrid VQE-SQD pipelines to CO₂ capture via metal-organic frameworks. Previously, NSF-funded research ($247K grant) on multivariate Goppa codes integrated into the McEliece cryptosystem, contributing to NIST post-quantum standardization.
